# CONFIDENTIAL: SBI SHORTFALL REPORT 1991-92
Date: April 14, 1992
To: Main Branch Manager, Mumbai
From: Internal Audit Division

Our reconciliation of government securities against the Physical Custody Register has revealed a critical shortfall of approximately ₹500 Crores in sGL account balances.

The discrepancies involve 'Ready Forward' transactions conducted with broker Harshad Shantilal Mehta. Specifically, our holdings of 11.5% 2010 GOI Bonds appear to have been transferred out without corresponding Bank Receipts (BRs) or legitimate delivery challans.

Immediate investigation into the role of the Securities Department is recommended.
